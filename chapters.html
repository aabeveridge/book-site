Doing Digital Visual Studies

Doing Digital Visual Studies: One Image, Multiple Methodologies

Edited by Laurie E. Gries, Aaron Beveridge, and Blake Hallinan

In taking up Shepard Fairey's Obama Hope design as a single object of study, Doing Digital Visual Studies experiments with various methodologies and methods to explore how “the digital” is inflecting upon and diversifying visual studies in inventive and productive ways.

Afterward: Methodological Experimentation in Digital Visual Studies

The "Afterward" by Beveridge and Gries focuses on lingering methodological questions raised by the collection. Deploying a diffractive approach, Beveridge and Gries identify research patterns in the collection, ultimately advocating for scholars to amplify experimental methodological play.
